# KDP Positioning

## Primary Positioning

`Fourteen Hours Apart` should be positioned as mature second-chance contemporary romance with literary romantic fiction appeal.

## Reader Promise

For readers who want:

- Adult emotional stakes
- Second chances after decades apart
- Long-distance intimacy
- Cross-cultural identity handled with restraint
- Romance shaped by career, family, time, and consequence

## Likely Reader Keywords

- second chance romance
- mature romance
- contemporary romance
- long distance romance
- Korean romance
- cross cultural romance
- later in life romance
- love after fifty
- romantic fiction
- Seoul romance
- New York romance

## Positioning Caution

Avoid presenting the project as memoir, autobiography, or a direct retelling of real events. The sales copy should promise a novel.
